Postpartum Assessment

Instructions: Check conditions if present, do not check if uncertain or not present

CMQCC High-Risk Checks:

Placenta previa or low lying placenta
Suspected placenta accreta/percreta
Hematocrit less than 30
Active bleeding (greater than show) on admission
Platelet count less than 100,000
Known coagulopathy

CMQCC Medium-Risk Checks:

Previous cesarean delivery or uterine surgery
Multiple gestation
Greater than 4 previous vaginal deliveries
Chorioamnionitis
History of peripartum hemorrhage
Large uterine leiomyoma
Macrosomia (estimated fetal weight > 4 kg)
Morbid Obesity (BMI greater than 35 kg/m^2)

Additional Maternal Antepartum Checks:

Age over 40
Arrhythmia
Asthma
Caucasian race
Cervical cerclage
Diabetes (type I, type II, gestational)
Clinical depression
Endometritis
First delivery
Heart failure
Heart disease (congenital, rheumatic, myocarditis/cardiomyopathy, valve disorder, artificial valves, or other)
Chronic hypertension
Lupus
Maternal infertility
Placental abruption
Polyhydroamnios
Preeclampsia
Renal abnormality (glomerulonephritis, pyelonephritis, lupus nephritis, nephrotic syndromes, nephrolithiasis, transplant, renal abscess, or other)
Infant renal insufficiency
Maternal acute renal failure
Sickle cell trait
Smoking (maternal smoking history)
Urinary tract infection

Additional Maternal Peripartum Checks:

Cesarean section
Preterm labor
Prolonged labor
Respiratory distress
Sickle cell crisis
